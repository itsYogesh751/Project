Tiger poulation is reducing

There are around 3,900 tigers worldwide, according to World Wildlife Fund, as of April 2016. The world has lost 95% of its tiger population since the beginning of the 20th century. The decline is attributed to illegal trade in tiger parts, hunting and habitat loss.

Some question and answers

Are Tigers in danger ?
Tigers are in great danger of extinction. around 95% of tigers have gone extinct in 20th century. Hunters still search for tigers .

why should we save tigers?
It is a top predator which is at the apex of the food chain and keeps the population of wild ungulates in check, thereby maintaining the balance between prey herbivores and the vegetation upon which they feed. Therefore, the presence of tigers in the forest is an indicator of the well being of the ecosystem.

How can we help to save tigers?
Donation can help to save tigers . This donation will be used to grow forest and natural habitat for tigers. Your small help can make very big change.
